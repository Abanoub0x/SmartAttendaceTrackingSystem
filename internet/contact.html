Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

What if I can't check in?

Make sure you're within the campus premises and have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact support.

How do I update my profile?

Go to your profile settings and click on the edit button to update your information.
